Getting Around

We manage transfers, local flights, and road travel so you can focus on the experience.

Transport Options

  • Airport transfers: Meet-and-greet with private transfers to lodges and hotels.
  • 4x4 Safari Vehicles: Comfortable game-viewing vehicles with experienced drivers/guides.
  • Domestic Flights: Scheduled and charter flights between key airstrips to save travel time.
  • Boat Transfers: For coastal journeys and island access in Lamu and other coastal areas.

All transport is arranged with safety and punctuality in mind. Tell us any mobility needs and we’ll accommodate them.
