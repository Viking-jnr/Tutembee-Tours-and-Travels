Discover Kenya with Local Experts

At Tutembee Tours & Travels we design journeys that connect you with Kenya's landscapes, wildlife, and vibrant cultures — responsibly and memorably.

Who We Are

Tutembee Tours & Travels is a locally-owned travel company founded by passionate Kenyan guides and hospitality professionals. We combine decades of on-the-ground experience with a love for storytelling to craft trips that feel personal, safe, and authentic.

Our Mission

We create thoughtful travel experiences that support local communities, conserve natural habitats, and leave you with lifelong memories. From sunrise safaris to market-food tours, our itineraries are flexible and built around what you value most.

What To Expect

  • Expert local guides: Knowledgeable, friendly guides who bring every destination to life.
  • Hand-picked stays: Comfortable lodges and boutique hotels chosen for location and service.
  • Responsible tourism: Partnerships that benefit communities and protect wildlife habitats.
  • Custom itineraries: Flexible plans for families, couples, solo travellers, and small groups.
  • Seamless logistics: Transfers, local flights, and transport handled so you can relax and explore.

Top Experiences

Whether you want classic safari sightings or immersive cultural moments, here are some favourites our travellers choose:

  1. Big Five safaris at world-renowned reserves
  2. Guided walks with Maasai and local communities
  3. Coastal beach escapes and Swahili food tours
  4. Birdwatching and nature photography trips
