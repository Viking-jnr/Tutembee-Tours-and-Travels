Events & Festivals

Experience Kenya's cultural heartbeat — vibrant festivals, music and seasonal wildlife events.

Annual Highlights

  • Great Migration (Maasai Mara): July–October — witness millions of wildebeest and zebra cross the Mara River.
  • Lamu Cultural Festival: November — dhow races, taarab music and Swahili culture.
  • Nairobi Marathon & Festival: Various dates — sport and community events.
  • Lake Turkana Cultural Festival: December — music, dance and cultural exchange.

We can schedule trips around key events — contact us for tailored dates and logistics.
