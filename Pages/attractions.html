Must-See Attractions in Kenya

From sweeping savannahs to turquoise coasts, Kenya is packed with experiences for every traveller.

Maasai Mara National Reserve

Witness the Great Migration, enjoy game drives, and meet Maasai communities.

Why go: Exceptional wildlife viewing and dramatic landscapes.

View Photos

Amboseli National Park

Famous for close-up views of African elephants with Mount Kilimanjaro as a backdrop.

Why go: Iconic photography and large elephant herds.

Laikipia & Samburu

Remote conservancies with unique species and community-led conservation projects.

Why go: Off-the-beaten-path luxury and rare wildlife.

Coastal Kenya — Diani & Lamu

White-sand beaches, Swahili culture, and world-class snorkeling.

Why go: Relaxation, water sports, and cultural exploration.

Mount Kenya

Hiking, scenic glaciers, and highland forests for active travellers.

Why go: Challenging treks and dramatic mountain scenery.

Nairobi — Urban & Cultural Highlights

Museums, vibrant markets, and conservation centres like the David Sheldrick Wildlife Trust.

Why go: Blend of city comforts and wildlife experiences.
